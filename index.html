Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take?

Most sites are live within 7 business days after discovery is complete and content is received. We'll always give you a clear timeline before we start.

How are the sites built?

Every site is 100% hand-coded with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. That means faster load times, cleaner structure, and no dependency on plugins or platforms that break.

What is on-page SEO?

It's the foundation that helps search engines understand your business — page titles, headings, meta descriptions, and structure. Without it, your site is invisible. With it, local customers can actually find you.

Is hosting included?

Yes. Hosting and domain setup are included in every package. We handle the technical setup so you don't have to think about it.

What counts as an edit?

Edits include text changes, image swaps, contact info updates, button fixes, and minor section adjustments. New pages, redesigns, and custom features are quoted separately.
